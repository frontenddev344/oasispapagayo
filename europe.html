Europe
Discover Europe’s Endless Charms

Europe delivers a mix of history, character, and landscapes that few places on earth can match. Ancient cities sit beside modern capitals. Mountains, coastlines, and countryside stretch across the continent. From regional dishes to local traditions, each destination offers its own story. Whether you're looking to relax, explore, or soak up culture, Europe has something for every kind of traveller.

Featured Hotels in Europe

Hotel Brunelleschi

Tucked into one of Florence’s oldest landmarks, Hotel Brunelleschi blends historical elegance with polished modern comfort. Its unbeatable location makes it a top pick for those looking to experience the city’s art, architecture, and culinary scene without compromise.

  • Florence, Italy
  • Restored medieval tower
  • Steps from Florence Cathedral
  • On-site gourmet restaurant
From $450 CAD / night

Pestana Palace

A converted palace with sweeping river views, Pestana Palace is a peaceful oasis tucked within Lisbon’s historic centre. Ideal for travellers who enjoy the finer things without being far from the heart of the city.

  • Lisbon, Portugal
  • 19th-century palace setting
  • Tropical gardens and outdoor pool
  • Spa and wellness centre
From $380 CAD / night
Book now

Hotel Casa Camper

This sleek, design-forward hotel offers a relaxed stay just steps from Las Ramblas. Hotel Casa Camper is known for its functional style, comfort-first approach, and subtle luxury that appeals to curious travellers and creatives alike.

  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Located in El Raval district
  • Rooftop terrace and honesty bar
  • Eco-conscious design
From $260 CAD / night
Book now

Hotel Le Meurice

Hotel Le Meurice has been a favourite among artists, writers, and royals for over a century. With its timeless style and unmatched location near the Louvre, it offers an elegant home base for exploring the best of Paris.

  • Paris, France
  • Facing the Tuileries Garden
  • Michelin-starred dining
  • Classic Parisian interiors
From $850 CAD / night
Book now

Canaves Oia Suites

Perched above Santorini’s caldera, Canaves Oia Suites offers dramatic sea views and peaceful luxury. Ideal for romantic escapes or solo travellers seeking quiet and beauty in one of Europe’s most photogenic settings.

  • Santorini, Greece
  • Private plunge pools
  • Caldera-facing suites
  • Traditional cave-style rooms
From $610 CAD / night
Book now

The Ritz London

An icon of British elegance, The Ritz delivers high-end service and old-school charm. Its location puts guests steps away from London’s major theatres, shops, and galleries, making it as convenient as it is impressive.

  • London, England
  • Central Piccadilly location
  • Afternoon tea in The Palm Court
  • Edwardian-style decor
From $960 CAD / night
