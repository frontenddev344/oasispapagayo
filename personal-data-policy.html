Personal Data Policy
At Oasis Papagayo, protecting your personal data is essential.

This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website and services. It is designed to keep you informed about your rights and our responsibilities.

1. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact details submitted through forms or newsletter sign-ups
  • Any other information you voluntarily share with us

We may also collect technical data automatically, such as IP addresses, browser type, and device information, to improve your experience.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal data is collected for specific purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending newsletters and promotional materials if you opt in
  • Enhancing website functionality and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on one or more of the following lawful grounds:

  • Your consent (e.g., subscribing to newsletters)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., providing requested information or services)
  • Compliance with legal requirements
  • Legitimate interests, such as improving our services

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined, or as required by law. When no longer needed, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Sharing and Transfers

Your personal data is not sold or rented. We may share it with trusted third-party service providers who support our operations (such as email services or analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements. Some data may be transferred internationally, but we ensure adequate protections are in place.

6. Data Security

We employ technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights concerning your personal data, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Requesting correction or deletion
  • Restricting or objecting to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawing consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@oasispapagayo.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analyse usage. Please see our Privacy Policy for details on managing these tools.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us to request deletion.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Personal Data Policy may be updated to reflect changes in law or our practices. Please review it periodically. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
